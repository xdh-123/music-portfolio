About Me

Hello, my name is Ziqi Li, and I am a composer and sound designer specializing in film scoring and multimedia projects. With a background in Computer Music, I have a passion for blending traditional symphonic elements with cutting-edge technology to create immersive and emotionally resonant compositions.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of contributing to several award-winning productions. Notable achievements include my work on films that earned recognition at the Cannes World Film Festival, Hollywood Gold Awards, and Vancouver International Movie Awards.

I began my journey in music at a young age, developing a strong foundation in classical training before shifting my focus to film scoring and sound design. My fascination with the emotional power of music in visual media started with the movie Up and its soundtrack, which inspired me to pursue a career in this field.
